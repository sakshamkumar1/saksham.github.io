About Me

My name is Saksham Kumar. Saksham means capable and Kumar means a prince.

I was born in Patna, a city in the Eastern part of India. I have spent most of my childhood here. I completed my higher secondary school and then, briefly moved to Rajasthan in the Western part of India for my high school. After my high school, I again shifted back to Patna and completed my bachelor's in engineering. Later, I moved to Kharagpur in West Bengal to pursue my master's degree.

I am currently specializing in the area of mechanical systems design and pursuing my M.Tech (Master of Technology) at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur.
